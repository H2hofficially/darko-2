06 // FAQ

Before you ask.

Is this manipulation?
No. It is literacy. Most operators arrive here because someone has been running these moves on them. Darko teaches you to see the move so you can decline it. Whether you ever run one yourself is your call, not the tool's.
How is Darko different from ChatGPT?
ChatGPT is trained to be agreeable, so in relational contexts it confirms whatever read you bring it. Darko applies a fixed canon — Greene, Cialdini, Bowlby, Gottman, Freud — to name what is actually being run, even when the answer is uncomfortable.
What can I actually use it for?
Reading the message you cannot decode. Catching your own anxious-protest replies before you send them. Spotting love-bombing in week one. Tracking phase changes across a multi-week thread. Naming attachment patterns in real time instead of weeks later.

Yes. Decodes are encrypted at rest and readable only by your account. Anonymous free-tier decodes are deleted within 24 hours. Content is never sold or shared with third parties.
