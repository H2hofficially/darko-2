# THE ARCHITECTURE OF PURSUIT: REVERSING THE TEXTUAL DYNAMIC

> The desire to "make her chase" is a symptom of a lost frame. Pursuit is a resource; its allocation dictates perceived value. Reclaim your leverage by understanding the underlying power mechanics.

---

## SIGNAL DETECTED

The signal you are responding to is her diminished textual investment. This manifests as delayed responses, minimal text length, infrequent initiation, or a general lack of engagement in your digital interactions. This behavior is not random; it is a clear indicator that your perceived value, relative to your current level of pursuit, has decreased. Your anxiety is a direct consequence of this perceived power imbalance.
